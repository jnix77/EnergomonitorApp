# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-27

This Privacy Policy describes how the Energomonitor Android App ("the App") handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

The App connects directly to the Energomonitor platform using the credentials or access tokens you provide. The App **does not** collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any third-party servers aside from the official Energomonitor API servers required to fetch and display your sensor metrics.

### Authentication Data
When you log in or authorize the App, your credentials are used solely to obtain an authorization token from the Energomonitor API. This token is stored securely on your device (using Android's preferences or DataStore) to maintain your session and fetch sensor data. The App does not send your username, password, or generated tokens anywhere else.

### Sensor Data
The App fetches your energy, temperature, power, gas, water, humidity, and CO2 data from the Energomonitor API to display it on your dashboard and home screen widgets. This data is only temporarily cached on your device (when necessary) to improve performance and provide updated widgets. 

## 2. Permissions

The App requests minimal permissions to function:
- **Internet Access:** Required to communicate with the Energomonitor API to fetch your data and dashboard information.

The App **does not** request unnecessary intrusive permissions (such as location, camera, microphone, or contacts).

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App communicates solely with the official Energomonitor platform. We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs within the App that would harvest or share your data.

## 4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your data, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. The App communicates with the Energomonitor API using secure, encrypted channels (HTTPS) to protect your authentication tokens and sensor data from unauthorized access during transmission.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by updating the Effective Date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
